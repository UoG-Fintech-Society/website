Our amazing workshop teams.


Blockchain and Cryptocurrencies workshop

The workshop team made an exceptional work providing easy to understand explanations on fundemental concepts around blockchain and cryptocurrencies. Namely, these include blockchain functionality, cryptography, smart contracts, ERC20 tokens, ICOs and regulations. Also an amazing speech was given around the big picture of blockchain by Mr. Dug Campbell The official report and presentation can be found in the resources section.


P2P Lending and Stock trading apps

P2P Lending and stock trading apps are a trending topic nowadays. The workshop team did an amazing job giving the background history, explaining the regulatory framework and illustrating the advantages and disadvantages around these topics. The workshop concluded by an extremely interesting speech around Fintech security by Dr. Greg Paul.
